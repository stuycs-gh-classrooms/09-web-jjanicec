In Project Sekai, there are fictional characters in fictional groups with fictional storylines. Different groups have focuses on different aesthetics and genres of music. The groups in the game are VIRTUAL SINGERS, Leoneed, MORE MORE JUMP, Vivid Bad Squad, WonderlandsxShowtime, and Nightcord 25:00. The VIRTUAL SINGERS are the only group not unique to the game, as they are a group of Vocaloids that are also popular outside the Project Sekai community. Every character in the game, except for the VIRTUAL SINGERS because they are Vocaloids, has a voice actor that sings and speaks in stories.

Here is a list of the characters in each group and a very brief description: VIRTUAL SINGERS

  • Hatsune Miku
  • Kagamine Rin
  • Kagamine Len
  • Megurine Luka
  • MEIKO
  • KAITO
The VIRTUAL SINGERS are Vocaloids and aren't the main characters of the story. They are also not in the "real world" of the game and its storylines.

Leoneed

  • Ichika Hoshino
  • Saki Tenma
  • Honami Mochizuki
  • Shiho Hinomori
Leoneed is a band that is centered around a theme of school and enjoying one's highschool life.

MORE MORE JUMP

  • Minori Hanasato
  • Haruka Kiritani
  • Airi Momoi
  • Shizuku Hinomori
MORE MORE JUMP is an idol group that aims to spread hope to their fans and people in general.

Vivid Bad Squad

  • Kohane Azusawa
  • An Shiraishi
  • Akito Shinonome
  • Toya Aoyagi
Vivid Bad Squad is a group of performers that have an aesthetic centered around street music and their goal is to surpass a legendary event called "RAD WEEKEND".

WonderlandsxShowtime

  • Tsukasa Tenma
  • Emu Otori
  • Nene Kusanagi
  • Rui Kamishiro
WonderlandsxShowtime is a group that performs in shows at an amusement park.

Nightcord 25:00

  • Kanade Yosaki
  • Mafuyu Asahina
  • Ena Shinonome
  • Mizuki Akiyama
Nightcord 25:00 is an online group that compose music and music videos together at 25:00 (1 AM). They are known in the community as the "emo group".
